Celebration of Chris Rocher's Life

Christopher "Chris" Owen Rocher, 49, passed away on May 5, 2023 due to of complications from cancer.
We are holding an informal, drop-in event to come together, share memories and celebrate Chris.

When?

Sunday, June 11th, between 1PM and 4PM

Where?

The Wooly , a spacious indoor venue in downtown Gainesville at 20 N Main St .

Where do I park?

There is street-side parking in the area, with a few larger city government lots a couple blocks away that are available on the weekend. These are highlighted in solid blue on the map below.

There are a few pay lots nearby as well, highlighted in green stripes on the map below.

What should I bring?

In lieu of flowers, please consider contributing to Mitchell's savings for the future, via Venmo or PayPal.

Otherwise, donations may be sent to Haven Hospice, a not-for-profit organization that helped support Chris during his cancer journey.
